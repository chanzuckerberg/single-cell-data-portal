# Join the CZ CELLxGENE User Community

<Image src={"/doc-site/7_join_cxg_user_community.png"} />

CZ CELLxGENE is guided by the principle of building with, not for. We love to hear from and connect with our community so we can learn to improve our tool and develop new features that meet your needs. Below are some ways to get in touch with us:

#### Join Our User Community on GitHub

Join our [GitHub community](https://github.com/chanzuckerberg/cellxgene) to connect with the CZ CELLxGENE team who can answer any questions you might have, review questions other users have posed, and connect with other scientists working with single cell data.

#### Send Us An Email

If GitHub is not your thing, but you still have questions or feedback, no sweat. Just send us a message and our team will get back to you within one working day: [cellxgene@chanzuckerberg.com](mailto:cellxgene@chanzuckerberg.com)

#### Sign Up for Feedback Sessions

We’re always thinking of ways to improve CZ CELLxGENE and we’re always looking for feedback on our latest ideas. Fill out [this form](https://airtable.com/shrJxB8X3ObDl2ECo) to be added to our database of users and we will reach out for our next feedback opportunity.
